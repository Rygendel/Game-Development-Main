Aeron:

These are the special concepts and texts which are used to endow things with special ideas beyond imagining by mortal beings. These transcend the universe, and are utterly new and greater than anything we can imagine. In some cases they can extend concepts of desirability, wish, desire, information, possess, good, and much more.

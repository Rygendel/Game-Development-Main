Sirions and Tirions:
---------------------

General Description:
---------------------
Similar to Aercyons, Sirions and Tirions are special objects which are extremely difficult to acquire. They are among the most powerful and abstract entities in the game, and can be used for essentially anything. They achieve Aeonar, and most have essences of Aenarion.
